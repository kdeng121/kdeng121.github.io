About

Hey there! I’m Kevin Troy Deng - a recent grad from Duke University with a B.S. in Computer Science and minor in Education. I have a passion for web development and improving the way that students learn. Currently, I'm a Software Engineer at Alarm.com, specializing in scaling up its smart home automation platform to support thousands of small to medium-sized businesses.

Two summers ago, I spent 8 weeks in the rural town of Zhuhai, China teaching middle school students English and Raas dance classes as part of the DukeEngage program. Through this experience, I was able to not only help students develop their own confidence and voice, but also discover energy and open-mindedness in myself as well. This past summer, I was a Software Engineer Intern at AppFolio, where I focused on API development and UI infrastructure for its text-messaging feature. I particularly was driven by how my contributions made a high impact on the sustainability of the software.

At Duke, I spent much of my time leading a discussion class as a Data Structures & Algorithms (CS201) Teaching Assistant as well as providing peers with resume and cover letter guidance as a Career Ambassador. I also learned a great amount as the Chief Technology Officer of Duke University Union, where I led the DevOps team to build software solutions and analyze attendance data to improve future events.

In my free time, I enjoy jammin’ out to All Time Low, building meaningful relationships, and attempting to beat my 100-meter sprint record. While I love teaching others as a CodeConnects instructor, I'm also obsessed with learning about random things, from how airports operate to how OLED displays are made. I’m in the process of becoming a virtuosic piano composer, so let me know if you'd like to start a band with me!
