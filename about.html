About

The T stands for Troy, in case you’re wondering.

So hey! I’m Kevin Troy Deng – a Junior at Duke University studying Computer Science and Education. I have a passion for web development and improving the way students learn. This past summer, I was immersed in the rural city of Zhuhai, China as part of a DukeEngage program, where my team and I taught middle school children English and the arts (I taught Raas dance). Through this experience, I learned the value of embracing confidence and open-mindedness, and I apply this attitude to my journey of combining tech and education.

At Duke, I invest my time in the Career Center. As a Career Ambassador, I provide students resume and cover letter guidance and convince them that their lives are not over just because they got rejected to Google’s internship program. The world needs more positivity, yeah?

In my free time, I enjoy jammin’ out to All Time Low, building meaningful relationships, and attempting to beat my 100-meter sprint record. I am obsessed with learning about random things, from how airports operate to how OLED displays are made. My dream of becoming of piano composer has been crushed recently; I am convinced that musical creativity takes practice and patience.
